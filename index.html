How to Install the Garmin Venu 2 Update

Step-by-Step Update Guide

  1. Charge Your Watch: Ensure your Venu 2 has at least 50% battery before starting.
  2. Connect to Garmin Connect: Open the Garmin Connect app on your paired smartphone.
  3. Check for Updates: Go to Settings > Device > System Update.
  4. Download the Update: If available, tap "Install Update" to begin downloading.
  5. Transfer to Your Watch: Keep your phone near your watch during the transfer process.
  6. Complete Installation: The watch will install the update automatically - don't interrupt this process.
  7. Restart Your Watch: After installation, your Venu 2 may restart to finalize the update.

Alternative Method via Wi-Fi: If your Venu 2 is connected to Wi-Fi, the update may download automatically when available. You'll receive a notification when it's ready to install.
